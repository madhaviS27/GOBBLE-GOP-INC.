HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE



Dodge the balls to stay alive in the game.

There is a timer in the game which reflects how long could you survive in the game.

Each time you collect a ball the wall shrinks but some percentage.

After the game is over, the timer value is your final score.





CONTROLS





Move your cursor in the left direction to move your player to the left.

Similarly, move your cursor in the right direction to move your player to the right.
